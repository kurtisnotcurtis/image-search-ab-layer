Image Search Abstraction Layer - FCC

This is a basic webapp that allows you to search Google for images that match your search term.

Enter a search term below and press submit, or enter your query into your browser's address bar like so:

https://img-search-ab-layer.glitch.me/api/imagesearch/YourQueryHere

Note that you can also append:

?offset=PageNumber

to display results on subsequent Google search pages.
